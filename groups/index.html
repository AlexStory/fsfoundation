Worldwide

Community for F#

Wherever you might be, Community for F# makes great F# content available to you, and provides resources so that you can organize your own local user group. We stream talks online, and make presentation recordings available. We also provide material that you can use in your own meetings, as well as advice on how to grow a dynamic local community.

Check out our website at c4fsharp.net, or find us on Twitter as @c4fsharp - and let us know how we can help!

North America

The San Francisco Bay Area F# User Group

SF# is a group of developers in the San Francisco Bay Area who have an interest in the F# programming language. Novices and experts are both welcome.

For more information, see the group page on meetup.

Detroit F# Meetup

A group of developers in Motown who are interested in learning to use F# to write better code. We meet once a month.

Chicago F# Users

The Chicago F# Users group is dedicated to the advancement of the F# language throughout the Chicago area. A virtual user group for Chinese F# programmer. The community uses QQ to exchange F# programming ideas in real time. The QQ group number is 61436709, which can be found by following the instructions. Please join and share up-to-date information, questions, and tips related to F#.
